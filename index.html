Our Legacy

O.N.S. OUTSOURCING SOLUTIONS was established in 2008 by Mr. Girijashanker Mishra and Mrs. Kavita Mishra with a singular vision: to address the growing demand for reliable outsourcing in domestic and international markets.

For nearly two decades, we have stood by our motto, "In Excellence We Trust." Today, led by Aditya Mishra, we continue to expand our legacy, incorporating modern innovations while maintaining the values of integrity and professionalism.

We position ourselves as a one-stop business partner for SMEs, startups, and large enterprises. We understand that a one-person army cannot handle every business process. We take that burden off your shoulders.
